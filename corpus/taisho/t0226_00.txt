<h4>Colophon</h4>
摩訶般若鈔經
前秦 曇摩蜱共竺佛念譯

共 5 卷

Condensed Version of the Mahāprajñāpāramitā Sūtra
(Mohe Bore Chao Jing);
Translated by Dharmapriya and Fo Nian in the Former Qin in 5 scrolls.

<h4>Summary</h4>
This is third translation of the Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines or the Aṣṭasāhasrikā Prajñāpāramitā (Lancaster, 1975).

<h4>Notes</h4>
Translated into Chinese in 382 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 5')
