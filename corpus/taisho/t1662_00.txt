菩提行經

Volume 32, No. 1662;
A Guide to the Bodhisattva Way of Life;

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 985 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1121'). Sanskrit
and Tibetan versions attribute this text to Śāntideva.
