<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha, surrounded by a large gathering of monks, is asked by Ananda to announce the new year. The Buddha instructs Maudgalyayana to summon all monks from across the universe to assemble. Upon gathering, the Buddha teaches the importance of moral purity and repentance for a meaningful new year celebration, emphasizing that true observance requires a pure mind, body, and speech, and that those who break the precepts cannot be saved even with the observance of the new year.
