<h4>Summary</h4>
The story tells of a wealthy man, Full-of-Wealth, seeking to marry Sumati, the daughter of Anathapindika. Anathapindika was hesitant because Sumati practiced Buddhism while Full-of-Wealth's family worshipped other gods and sacrificed animals. Eventually, the marriage proceeds, and Sumati uses her spiritual powers to convert the people of her new city to Buddhism, after encountering hostility from local Brahmins.
