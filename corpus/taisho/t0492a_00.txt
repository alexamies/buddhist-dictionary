<h4>Summary</h4>
This text recounts a conversation where a disciple asks Buddha why some who serve Buddha prosper while others decline. Buddha explains that true devotion, adherence to precepts, and virtuous conduct lead to blessings and heavenly protection, while superficial or malicious practices result in misfortune and suffering. The text further discusses the consequences of harming others, disrespecting teachers, and the importance of upholding Buddhist principles even amidst worldly affairs. It concludes with a plea for Buddha to remain in the world and guide future generations, emphasizing the rarity of encountering Buddhist teachings and the dire consequences of disbelief.
