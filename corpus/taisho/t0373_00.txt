<h4>Summary</h4>
This text describes the vows and virtues of a past monk who became a Buddha with immeasurable light and life. It details the pure and joyful land he resides in, filled with fragrant trees and rivers that sing like scriptures. The poem emphasizes the importance of faith in this Buddha, warning against doubt and encouraging devotion to be reborn in his blessed realm. Finally, it urges followers to protect the Buddha's teachings, especially in times of decline, and to practice diligently for boundless blessings.
