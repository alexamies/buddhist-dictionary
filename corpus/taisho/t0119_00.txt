<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha was residing in Jetavana when he heard about Angulimala, a ruthless murderer terrorizing the kingdom. The Buddha then went to find Angulimala, who tried to kill him but couldn't catch up to him. Angulimala was then converted by the Buddha's teachings, gave up his violent ways, and became a monk. Later, after struggling with the consequences of his past actions, Angulimala became an Arhat.
