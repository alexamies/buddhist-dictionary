The Lotus Sutra or Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Satanfentuoli Jing)
by an unknown translator in 1 scroll.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 265-317 and Sanskrit title from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 115').
This is an early translation of the Lotus Sūtra.
