<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha was staying in a monastery when a lay disciple invited him and his monks for a meal. Before the meal, the Buddha revealed that two powerful dragon kings were creating darkness due to their anger. The disciple, Maudgalyayana, intervened and subdued the dragons by demonstrating his spiritual power, eventually leading them to repent and take Buddhist vows. The Buddha then explained the situation to the lay disciple, praising Maudgalyayana's abilities and blessing the lay disciple's generosity.
