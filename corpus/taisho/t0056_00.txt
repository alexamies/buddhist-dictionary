<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha taught that those who cling to the elements (earth, water, fire, wind) or states of being (heavens, Brahma heavens) as "self" are ignorant. True understanding comes from not delighting in these elements or states, nor identifying them as "self." Furthermore, enlightened beings transcend the cycle of birth and death by understanding and not clinging to these concepts as "self." Ultimately, the Buddha emphasizes detaching oneself from these attachments to achieve true knowledge and liberation.
