<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 30 冊　No. 1575

六十頌如理論
龍樹菩薩造 宋 施護譯

共 1 卷

Volume 30, No. 1575;
Yuktiṣaṣṭikākārikā (Sixty Verses of Reasoning);
Composed by Nāgārjuna, translated by Dānapāla in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 1007 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 1441').
English title from Edelglass and Garfield (Edelglass and Garfield 2009, loc. 625).

<h4>English translation</h4>
Tola, F. and Dragonetti, C. 1983, “The Yuktiṣaṣṭikakārikā of Nāgārjuna,” <i>Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies</i>, pp. 94-123.
