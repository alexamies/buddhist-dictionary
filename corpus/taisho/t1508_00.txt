<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 25 冊　No. 1508

阿含口解十二因緣經
後漢 安玄共嚴佛調譯

共 1 卷

Volume 25, No. 1508;
Discourse of the Explanation of the Twelve Nidanas (Ahan Kou Jie Shi Er Yinyuan Jing);
Translated by An Xuan and Yan Fotiao in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Explains the chain of causation, the functions of the body, and the elements as the causes of diseases

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 181 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1023').
Wrongly attributed to An Xuan. It should be attributed to An Shigao (Zürcher 2013, p. 442).
