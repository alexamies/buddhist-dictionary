Sutra on the Spell for the Teeth;

Translated by Tan Wulan in the Eastern Jin in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text invokes the names of the Buddha, Dharma, Sangha, and prominent disciples, focusing on a dental ailment. The text addresses a "worm king" residing in a tooth, commanding it and its followers to cease afflicting the tooth and root. Disobedience is threatened with dire consequences, invoking a curse to ensure compliance. The ultimate goal is to achieve the desired outcome through the recited mantra, blessed by the divine.
