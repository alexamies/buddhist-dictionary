<h4>Colophon</h4>
僧伽羅剎所集經
符秦 僧伽跋澄等譯

共 3 卷

Scriptures Compiled by Saṃgharakṣa
(Sengjia Luocha Suo Ji Jing);
Translated by Saṅghabhuti in 3 scrolls in the Fu Qin

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 385 from Lancaster ('K 985'). Radich (2017, p. 7) writes that this text is wrongly attributed to Saṅghabhuti. It should be attributed to Zhu Fonian.
