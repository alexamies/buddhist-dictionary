<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2160

傳教大師將來越州錄
日本 最澄撰
共 1 卷

Volume 55, No. 2160;
Chuanjiao Dashi Jiang Lai Yuezhou Lu;
Dengyōdaishi shōrai esshūroku;
Compiled by Japanese monastic Saichō in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a catalog of esoteric works brought back to Japan from China by
Saichō (767–822).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text is not included in the Korean canon
