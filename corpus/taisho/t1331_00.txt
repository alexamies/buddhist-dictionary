佛說灌頂經

Volume 21, No. 1331;

Fo Shuo Guan Ding Jing;

Consecration Sutra;

Abhiseka Sutra;

<h4>Other names</h4>
Japanese: Kanjōkyō

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).
