<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 20 冊　No. 1113A

觀自在菩薩廣大圓滿無礙大悲心陀羅尼

高麗 指空校

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 20, No. 1113A;

Guanzizai Pusa Guangda Yuanman Wu'ai Dabei Xin Tuoluoni;

Avalokitesvara bodhisattva Perfect and Unimpeded Great Compassion Dharani;

Taught by Dhyānabhadra in the Korean Goryeo in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Gives the text of the dharani in Chinese transliteration.

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: 朝鮮弘治九年刊眞言集.
