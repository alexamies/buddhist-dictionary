<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說仁王般若波羅蜜經
後秦 鳩摩羅什譯

共 2 卷

Scripture for Humane Kings;
Translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin dynasty in 2 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Thought to be an apocryphal sūtra (BL 'Renwang Jing'; Swanson 1989, p. 46)

<h4>English translation</h4>
Conze, Edward 1993, <i>Perfect Wisdom: The Short Prajñāpāramitā Texts</i>, Kindle edition, Totnes: Buddhist Publishing Group, loc. 3462-3541. From Sanskrit.
