異部宗輪論

Volume 49, No. 	2031;
Yi Bu Zong Lun Lun;
Samayabhedoparacanacakra (The Cycle of the Formation of the Schismatic Doctrines);
Composed by Vasumitra, translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes early Indian Buddhist schools
