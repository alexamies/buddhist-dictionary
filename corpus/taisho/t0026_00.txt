Middle Length Discourses
(Zhong Ahan Jing);
Translated by Gautama Saṅghadeva in the Eastern Jin in 60 scrolls.<br/>

<h4>Summary</h4>
Approximately relates to the Majjhima Nikāya of the Pāli Canon. Many of the sūtras in Madhyamāgama are found in the Aṅguttara Nikāya (Numerical Discourses of the Buddha).

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
madhyamāgama
