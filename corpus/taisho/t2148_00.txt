<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2148

眾經目錄
唐 靜泰撰

共 5 卷

Volume 55, No. 2148;

Zhong Jing Mulu;

Tang Catalog of Sutras and Sastras from Eastern Capital Da Jing Ai Monastery;

Composed by Jing Tai in the Tang in 5 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
靜泰錄 Jing Tai Catalog

<h4>Summary</h4>
A catalog of the Chinese Buddhist canon produced in the Tang dynasty by imperial order.
