<h4>Colophon</h4>
勝天王般若波羅蜜經
陳 月婆首那譯

共 7 卷

Questions of the Deva King Pravara;
Suvikrāntavikrāmiparipṛcchāprajñāpāramitāsūtra;
Translated by Upaśūnya in the Chen dynasty in 7 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Upaśūnya 月婆首那 in 565, T 231 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 8').

<h4>English translation</h4>
Conze, Edward 1993, <i>Perfect Wisdom: The Short Prajñāpāramitā Texts</i>, Kindle edition, Totnes: Buddhist Publishing Group, loc. 179-1773. From Sanskrit.
