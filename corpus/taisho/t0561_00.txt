<h4>Summary</h4>
While in a kingdom, the Buddha was approached by a poor old woman who questioned him about the origins and destinations of life, the senses, and the elements. The Buddha explained that all things arise and cease due to causes and conditions, lacking inherent origin or destination, using analogies like fire from wood and sound from a drum. The woman, enlightened by the Buddha's teachings, rejoiced and later, at the end of her life, will be reborn in Amitabha Buddha's realm, eventually becoming a Buddha herself.
