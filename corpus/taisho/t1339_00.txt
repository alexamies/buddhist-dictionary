大方等陀羅尼經

北涼 法眾譯

共 4 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1339;

Pratyutpannabuddhasammukhāvasthitasamādhisūtra (Dafang Deng Tuoluoni Jing);

Translated by Fa Zhong in the Northern Liang in 4 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
Relations to questions asked of the Buddha by Mañjuśrī and following dhāraṇīs and the Buddha's description of their merits.
