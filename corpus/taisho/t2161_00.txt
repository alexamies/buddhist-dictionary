<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2161

御請來目錄
日本 空海撰
共 1 卷

Volume 55, No. 2161;
Shōrai Mokuroku;
Compiled by Japanese monastic Kūkai in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a catalog of esoteric works and other items brought back to Japan
from China by Kūkai (774–835).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text is not included in the Korean canon
