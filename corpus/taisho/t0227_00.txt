<h4>Colophon</h4>
小品般若波羅蜜經
後秦 鳩摩羅什譯
共 10 卷
<br/>

Sutra of Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines;
Xiaopin Boreiluomi Jing,
translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin (384－417) in 10 scrolls.

<h4>Summary</h4>
This sūtra is a version of the Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra or Aṣṭa for short, is thought to be the earliest prajñāpāramitāsūtra sūtra. (Buswell and Lopez, 2014, 'Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitā')
