後秦 鳩摩羅什譯

<br/>

The Perfection of Wisdom in 25,000 Lines (Pañcaviṃśatisāhasrikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra),
Translated by Kumārajīva in the Later Qin (384－417) in 27 scrolls.

<h4>English Translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
This sūtra, is one of the three key large prajñāpāramitāsūtras. The other two are the Aṣṭasāhasrikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra or or Perfection of Wisdom in Eight Thousand Lines 小品般若波羅蜜經, and The Large Sūtra Perfection of Wisdom 大般若波羅蜜多經 (Buswell and Lopez, 2014, 'Pañcaviṃśatisāhasrikāprajñāpāramitāsūtra').

<h4>Notes</h4>
Translated in 404 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 3')
