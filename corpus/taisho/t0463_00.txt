<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a sutra where the Buddha is residing in Jetavana Vihara in Sravasti. He radiates golden light and reveals auspicious signs foretelling the future Nirvana of Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva. The Buddha describes Mañjuśrī's extraordinary birth, powers, and future activities, emphasizing his role in benefiting sentient beings and teaching the Dharma, particularly in the Himalayas where he will teach the twelve-fold scriptural division to five hundred hermits. The Buddha also elaborates on the benefits of remembering, venerating, and contemplating Mañjuśrī, leading to the purification of karmic obstacles and eventual enlightenment.
