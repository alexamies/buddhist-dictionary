<h4>Colophon</h4>
仁王護國般若波羅蜜多經
唐 不空譯

共 2 卷

The Prajnaparamita Sutra Explaining How Benevolent Kings may Protect their Countries;
Renwang Huguo Boreluomi Jing;
Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang dynasty in 2 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
The text is similar in content to T 245 佛说仁王般若般若波罗蜜经 'Scripture for Humane Kings' and also believed to be apocryphal. (BL 'Renwang Huguo Boreluomi Jing'; Swanson, 1989, p. 45)
