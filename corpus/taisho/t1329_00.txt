Sutra on the Spell for Children;

Translated by Tan Wulan in the Eastern Jin in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text presents a Buddhist mantra intended for healing children. The mantra itself consists of a series of sounds and phrases. It is believed that reciting the mantra seven times will cure ailments like headaches and stomachaches in children. The text also invokes the names of past Buddhas and their disciples, seeking their blessings to ensure the mantra's efficacy.
