<h4>Summary</h4>
This text recounts the final days and passing of the Buddha. It details his interactions with various individuals, including kings, disciples, and a late convert, and the instructions he imparts. After the Buddha's death, the text describes the elaborate funeral arrangements, the distribution of his relics, and the events surrounding the compilation of his teachings. These teachings emphasize the importance of ethical conduct, mindfulness, and wisdom as paths to liberation from suffering.
