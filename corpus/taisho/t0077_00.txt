<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha is residing in the Jeta Grove when a monk named Luo-ye Qiang-qi is visited by a radiant celestial being who recites a verse about not dwelling on the past or future, but is unable to explain its meaning. The celestial being directs Luo-ye Qiang-qi to the Buddha to learn the verse's true significance. Luo-ye Qiang-qi travels to the Buddha, recounts his encounter, and asks the Buddha to explain the verse for the benefit of all monks. The Buddha then elucidates the verse, explaining how to avoid attachment to the past, future, and present.
