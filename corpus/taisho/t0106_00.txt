<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說水沫所漂經
東晉 竺曇無蘭譯

共 1 卷

Sutra on Foam Floating in the Water
(Fo Shuo Shui Mo Suo Piao Jing);
Translated by Tan Wulan in the Eastern Jin in 1 scroll

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Phena Sutta

<h4>Notes</h4>
Relation to the Pāli Canon from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 733'; Saṃyutta Nikāya 22.95)

<h4>English translations</h4>
From the Pāli (Bodhi 2005; Thanissaro 1999)
