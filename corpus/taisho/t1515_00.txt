金剛般若波羅蜜經破取著不壞假名論
功德施菩薩造 唐 地婆訶羅等譯

共 2 卷

Volume 25, No. 1515
Jingang Boreboluomi Jing Po Qu Zhe Bu Huai Jia Ming Lun
Composed by a bodhisattvas with great merit, translated by Divākara and others in the Song in 2 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
