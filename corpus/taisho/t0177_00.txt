<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha recounts the story of a prince named Mahāmati (大意) who, upon birth, vowed to alleviate the suffering of the world through generosity. He journeys to sea to collect treasures for distribution and encounters multiple jeweled cities, each guarded by a serpent, where he obtains luminous pearls. When a sea god steals the pearls, Great Intent's determination to retrieve them moves even the gods, leading to their return and enabling him to fulfill his vow of universal generosity.
