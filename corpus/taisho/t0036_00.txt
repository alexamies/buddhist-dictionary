<h4>Colophon</h4>
本相猗致經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

Sutra on the Causes of Forms of Existence
(Ben Xiang Yi Zhi Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Latter Han in 1 scroll.

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes a causal series leading from “bad company”, “lack of”, “improper thoughts,” ...

<h4>Notes</h4>
Relation to Korean canon from Lancaster (2004, 'K 674')
