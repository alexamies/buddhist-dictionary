<h4>Colophon</h4>
妙法蓮華經
姚秦 鳩摩羅什譯

共 7 卷
<br/>

The Lotus Sūtra; Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra (Miaofa Lianhua Jing),
Translated by Kumārajīva in 7 scrolls in the Later Qin

<h4>Summary</h4>
This is the popular translation of the Lotus Sūtra.
