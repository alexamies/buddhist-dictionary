<h4>Colophon</h4>
是法非法經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

Sutra on True Dharmas and False Dharmas
(Shi Fa Fei Fa Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Correspondence with Sappurisasutta in the Pāli Canon and sūtra translation date 148-170 from Lancaster. (2004, 'K 690')

<h4>English translations</h4>
From the Pāli (Majjhima Nikāya 113)
