<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2151

古今譯經圖紀
唐 靖邁撰

共 4 卷

Volume 55, No. 2151;

Gu Jin Yi Jing Tu Ji;

Notes on the Illustrations to the Old and Modern Translations of the Scriptures;

Composed by Jing Mai in the Tang in 4 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
譯經圖紀 Notes on the Illustrations to the Translations of the Scriptures

<h4>Summary</h4>
A catalog explaining portaits of famous Buddhist figures displayed in Xuang Zang's translation court.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 664-665 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 1059')
