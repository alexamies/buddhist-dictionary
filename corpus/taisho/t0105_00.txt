<h4>Colophon</h4>
五陰譬喻經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

Sūtra on Similes for the Five Aggregates (Wuyin Piyu Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Stories illustrating the unreality of the five aggregates of a person

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 148-170 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 743'). Zürcher (2013, p. 444) states that the attribution to An Shigao is doubtful.
