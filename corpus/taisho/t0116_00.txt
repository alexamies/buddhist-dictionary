From the Pāli (Bodhi 2012)

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha, residing in the Jetavana Vihara, answers Ananda's question about whether there is a fragrance that can travel against the wind. He explains that the true fragrance is the virtue of individuals who practice the ten good deeds, showing filial piety, respect for the Triple Gem, and kindness. The Buddha emphasizes that this ethical fragrance surpasses all other fragrances, spreading far and wide, bringing benefits to all and leading to enlightenment.
