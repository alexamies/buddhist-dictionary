五母子經
吳 支謙譯

Volume 14, No. 555a;
Sutra on the Child with Five Mothers
(Wu Mu Zi Jing);
Translated by Zhi Qian in the Wu in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
The child remembers her mothers from five previous lives.
