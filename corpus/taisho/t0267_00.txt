<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha is in a garden in the country of Savatthi, surrounded by monks and bodhisattvas, teaching them about the nature of reality and the path to enlightenment. A variety of beings, including gods and demons, express their understanding of the teachings and their commitment to the Buddhist path. The sutra emphasizes the importance of understanding emptiness and non-attachment, even in the face of the complexities and challenges of the world.
