七佛八菩薩所說大陀羅尼神呪經

 失譯

Volume 21, No. 1332;

Divine Spells and Great Dhāraṇīs Taught by the Seven Buddhas and Eight Bodhisattvas (Qi Fo Ba Pusa Suo Shuo Da Tuoluoni Shen Zhou Jing);

Translated by Unknown in 4 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a collection of dhāraṇīs attributed to the seven Buddhas and eight bodhisattvas in the title. It was originally a single scroll. It was later expanded into four scrolls.
