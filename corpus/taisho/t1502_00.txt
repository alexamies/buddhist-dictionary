<h4>Summary</h4>
This text outlines the precepts and practices of the Bodhisattva fast. It details the vows taken during the fast, including abstaining from personal adornment, entertainment, luxurious living, eating after midday, handling money or valuables, riding vehicles, and mistreating others. The text also emphasizes mindfulness, including contemplation of past, present, and future Buddhas, the practice of paramitas (perfections), and the aspiration to liberate beings from suffering. Finally, it describes specific dates for observing and ending the fast.
