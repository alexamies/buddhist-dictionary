<h4>Summary</h4>
This text recounts the story of Samagga, a wise woman from a Buddhist kingdom, who marries the king of a heretical nation. The king, proud of his own wisdom and devoted to naked ascetics, is challenged by Samagga's superior knowledge of Buddhism. She eventually invites the Buddha and his disciples to her husband's kingdom, leading to the conversion of many to Buddhism after they are convinced by the Buddha's miraculous display of power and teachings.
