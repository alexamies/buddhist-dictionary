<h4>Summary</h4>
A man traveling abroad breaks his vow to abstain from alcohol and is banished by his parents. He finds refuge in another country, where the host worships three powerful but malevolent spirits. Through the traveler's adherence to the remaining four of the five precepts of Buddhism, the spirits are driven away, and the host is inspired to take refuge in the Buddha. The duo later encounters 498 people, and they all return to the Buddha and become enlightened.
