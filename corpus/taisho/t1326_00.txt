Volume 21, No. 1326;

Fo Shuo Zhou Shi Qi Bing Jing;

Mantra for Curing Illness Sutra;

Translated by Tan Wulan in the Eastern Jin in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
One of a group of texts relating to healing sickness (T 1324-1330).
