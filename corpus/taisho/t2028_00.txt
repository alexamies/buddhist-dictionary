<h4>Summary</h4>
This text describes the decline of Buddhist teachings and morality in a future era.  It foretells corrupt monks prioritizing personal gain and worldly affairs over spiritual practice, leading to societal decay and natural disasters.  These degenerate conditions culminate in wars and the destruction of Buddhist sites by rulers, followed by a final gathering of monks marked by conflict and the violent end of virtuous figures. The text urges diligence in practicing the true Dharma to avoid suffering in this future time, emphasizing that earnest effort can overcome these predicted hardships.
