<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2145

出三藏記集
梁 僧祐撰

共 15 卷

Chu San Zang Ji Ji;
Collection of Records Concerning the Chinese Buddhist Canon;
Volume 55, No. 2145;
Compiled by Seng You in the Liang in 15 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
The oldest extant catalog of the Chinese Buddhist canon.
