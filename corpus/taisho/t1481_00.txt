佛說五恐怖世經
宋 沮渠京聲譯

共 1 卷

Volume 24, No. 1481;
Sutra of the Five Fears
(Fo Shuo Wu Kongbu Shi Jing);
Translated by Juqu Jingsheng in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 455- from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 841')

<h4>English Translations</h4>
None

<h4>Summary</h4>
The Buddha warns his disciples about five future dangers that will arise among monks. These include monks who lack
discipline, morality, and wisdom, leading others astray with false teachings. Some will disregard the true Dharma,
distort the scriptures, and prefer eloquent but misleading words over genuine wisdom.
