Volume 21, No. 1244;

Ātānātīyasūtra (Pishamen Tian Wang Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Other names</h4>
Vaisravana Sutra

<h4>Summary</h4>
This text includes Siddhaṃ script and Chinese transliteration for a mantra as well as ritual instructions.
