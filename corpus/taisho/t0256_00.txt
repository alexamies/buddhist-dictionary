唐梵翻對字音般若波羅蜜多心經
共 1 卷

Chinese-Sanskrit Phonetic Comparison for the Prajñāpāramitāhṛdayasūtra

<h4>Notes</h4>
Compares Chinese text with transliteration of the Sanskrit, circa 602-664. (FGS OBD '唐梵翻對字音般若波羅蜜多心經'; T 256)
