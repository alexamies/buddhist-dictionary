<h4>Summary</h4>
This text details a discourse by the Buddha on the pure conduct (淨行) for laywomen (優婆夷), including their past life connections to the Buddha, qualities of pure conduct, and various practices to cultivate. It outlines ten aspects of practice, fifty areas of learning for a Bodhisattva, and ways to generate the light and physical characteristics of a Buddha. The discourse ends with descriptions of sixteen miraculous signs at the Bodhisattva's birth and the benefits of faith in the Buddha's teachings.
