<h4>Summary</h4>
This text is a Buddhist scripture detailing the practices and thoughts of Bodhisattvas seeking Buddhahood. It outlines specific actions and corresponding mental intentions that Bodhisattvas cultivate to benefit all beings, envisioning that all sentient beings will attain enlightenment and escape suffering. The scripture also depicts Buddha's ability to manifest simultaneously in multiple realms, emphasizing his omnipresence and the universal potential for enlightenment. The story describes how the Buddha was asked to visit and was greeted by many celestial beings and Bodhisattvas, demonstrating the widespread devotion to Buddhism.
