<h4>Colophon</h4>
普法義經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

The Universal Meaning of the Dharma (Pu Fa Yi Jing);
Arthavighuṣṭasūtra;
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Lists of series of factors either conducive or obstructive to liberation.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 152 and Sanskrit title from Lancaster (2004, 'K 717')
