<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 55 冊　No. 2149

大唐內典錄
唐 道宣撰

共 10 卷

Volume 55, No. 2149;

Da Tang Nei Dian Lu;

Catalog of the Inner Canon of the Great Tang Dynasty;

Composed by Dao Xuan in the Tang in 10 scrolls

<h4>Summary</h4>
An important catalog of texts contained in the Chinese Buddhist canon in the early Tang.
