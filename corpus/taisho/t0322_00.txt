法鏡經
後漢 安玄譯

共 1 卷

Volume 12, No. 322;
Sutra of the Dharma Mirror (Fa Jing Jing);
Translated by An Xuan in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Ugradattaparipṛcchā

<h4>Summary</h4>
A Mahayana narrative of the Buddha's explaination of the duties of the lay believer to the lay person Ugradatta

<h4>Notes</h4>
