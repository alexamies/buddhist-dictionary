<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 30 冊　No. 1581

菩薩地持經
北涼 曇無讖譯

共 10 卷

Volume 30, No. 1581;
Bodhisattvabhūmi (Pusa Xing Chi Jing);
Translated by Dharmakṣema in the Northern Liang in 10 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
Sutra on Bodhisattva Stages; The Bodhisattava Path to Unsurpassed Enlightenment

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 414-426 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 523'). The Tibetan version of the text is elsewhere attributed to Asaṅga.

<h4>English Translations</h4>
None
