Volume 13, No. 418
Bhadrapālasūtra (Ban Zhou Sanmei Jing)
Translated by Lokakṣema in the Later Han in 3 scroll(s)

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the power of this samādhi which enables a devotee to visualize Amitābha.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Sanskrit title and date 179 from Lancaster (Lancaster 2004, 'K 67'). The title Bhadrapālasūtra is a transcription of Pratyutpanna-samādhi-sūtra.

<h4>English translations</h4>
Swanson, Paul 2017, <i>Clear Serenity, Quiet Insight</i>, Honolulu: University of Hawaii Press, Vol.3: pp. 1618-1629 (partial translation).
