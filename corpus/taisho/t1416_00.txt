<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 21 冊　No. 1416

金剛摧碎陀羅尼

宋 慈賢譯

共 1 卷
<br/>

Volume 21, No. 1416;

Jingang Cui Sui Tuoluoni;

Dharani on the Vajra able to Destroy Hindrances;

Translated by Ci Xian in the Song in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸陀羅尼經類 dharani sutras group (1331–1420).

<h4>Notes</h4>
This text does not appear in the Korean canon. A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞明本, 此經麗宋元三本倶缺 Source: Ming Canpn; this text is missing from the Korean, Song, and Yuan canons.
