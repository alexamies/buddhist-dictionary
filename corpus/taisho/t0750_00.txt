None

<h4>Summary</h4>
Once there was a boy, who became a monk’s disciple at the age of seven, diligently studying scriptures and serving his teacher in the mountains.
By age eight, he attained Arhatship, gaining supernatural abilities such as flying, seeing all things, hearing across realms, and knowing past lives.
