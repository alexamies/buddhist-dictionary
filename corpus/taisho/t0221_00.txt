<h4>Colophon</h4>
放光般若經
西晉 無羅叉譯
共 20 卷

Fang Guang Bore Jing
Translated by Mokṣala in the Western Jin (265-316) in 20 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated in 291. (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 2')

English translations: none
