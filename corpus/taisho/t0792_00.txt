第 17 冊　No. 792

佛說法受塵經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

Volume 17, No. 792;
Sutra on Suffering from Defilements
(Fo Shuo Fa Shou Chen Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Explain how sexual desire obstructs the path to insight and leads to suffereing.
