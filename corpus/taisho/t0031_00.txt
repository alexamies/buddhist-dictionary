<h4>Colophon</h4>
一切流攝守因經
後漢 安世高譯

共 1 卷

Sutra on Guarding Against the Causes of all the Taints
(Yiqie Liu She Shou Yin Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 1 scroll.
