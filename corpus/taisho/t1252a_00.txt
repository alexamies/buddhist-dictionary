Volume 21, No. 1252A;

Sutra on the Twelve Names of Mahāśrī (Fo Shuo Da Jixiang Tian Nu Shi Er Minghao Jing);

Translated by Amoghavajra in the Tang in 1 scroll

<h4>Alternative Titles</h4>
Mahāśrīsūtra

<h4>Summary</h4>
An esoteric text in the 諸天等儀軌類 deva ritual manuals group (1244–1330).

<h4>Notes</h4>
A Taishō footnote for this text reads: ＜原＞麗本 Source: Korean canon.
