金七十論

Volume 54, No. 2137;
Commentary on Samkhya Karika (Jin Qi Shi Lun);
Translated by Paramārtha in the Chen in 3 scrolls

<h4>Other Titles</h4>
僧佉論 Commentary on Samkhya Karika; 迦毘羅論 Commentary on Samkhya Karika

<h4>Summary</h4>
A commentary by Gauḍapāda on 僧佉頌 Samkhya Karika composed by Isvara Krishna, a central text of the orthodox Hindu Samkhya school.
