<h4>Summary</h4>
Ananda has seven dreams and asks Buddha about their meaning. Buddha explains that the dreams foretell the decline of Buddhist teachings and morality in a future degenerate age. The dreams depict conflicts within the monastic community, disrespect towards monks, and the eventual corruption of Buddhist practice. However, some dreams also symbolize Ananda's important role in preserving the teachings and the eventual weakening of the Dharma.
