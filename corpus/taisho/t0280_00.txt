<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說兜沙經
後漢 支婁迦讖譯

共 1 卷

Tusara Sutra (Dousha Jing);
Translated by Lokakṣema in the Later Han in 1 scroll

<h4>Summary</h4>
Describes the miraculous manifestation of Buddhas and bodhisattvas from all directions; the title is not explained.

<h4>Notes</h4>
Date 147 from Lancaster (2004, 'K 90')
