<h4>Summary</h4>
The text recounts the story of a woman who repeatedly requests the Buddha to allow women to ordain and attain enlightenment. Initially, the Buddha refuses, citing concerns about the longevity of the monastic order. However, after intervention and highlighting the woman's past kindness to him, the Buddha eventually agrees, but only under the condition that women adhere to eight strict rules and obligations.
