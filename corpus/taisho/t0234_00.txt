<h4>Colophon</h4>
佛說濡首菩薩無上清淨分衛經
宋 翔公譯

共 2 卷

Questions of Nāgaśrī;
Nāgaśrīpāripṛcchāsūtra;
Translated by Xiang Gong in the Liu Song dynasty in 2 scrolls.

<h4>Notes</h4>
A sūtra in the prajñāpāramitā tradition translated by Xiang Gong 420-479, T 234 (Lancaster, 2004, 'K 12').

<h4>English translation</h4>
Conze, Edward 1993, <i>Perfect Wisdom: The Short Prajñāpāramitā Texts</i>, Kindle edition, Totnes: Buddhist Publishing Group, loc. 3462-3541.
