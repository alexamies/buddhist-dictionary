阿毘達磨集異門足論

Volume 26, No. 1536;
Abhidharmasaṅgītīparyāyapādaśāstra (Apidamo Ji Yi Men Zu Lun);
Spoken by Śariputra, translated by Xuanzang in the Tang in 20 scrolls

<h4>Oher Titles</h4>
Saṃgīti-paryāya, Treatise on Pronouncements

<h4>Summary</h4>
A discussion of the Saṃgīti Sūtra, an early Sarvastivada Abhidharma text.
