<h4>Colophon</h4>
第 15 冊　No. 0603

陰持入經
後漢 安世高譯

共 2 卷

Volume 15, No. 603;
Sutra on the Skandha, the Dhātu, and the Ayatana (Yin Chi Ru Jing);
Translated by An Shigao in the Later Han in 2 scrolls;

<h4>Summary</h4>
A compendium discussing with perception, the four bases of spiritual power, the five powers, and so on.

<h4>Notes</h4>
